About Me

Background

I've recently discovered how much of a passion I have for developing and creating websites and different types of web content. It allows me to take from my creative side and merge that with my knowledge about computers which to me, is the best of both worlds. I have been learning and practicing mostly front-end development at the start of 2021, and have been tackling different varieties of projects to test and improve my skills while developing websites and various types of emails.

Since the beginning of the year I've been taking web developing classes to learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I've created various websites and emails as a result of those classes and have also learned to use other programs such as Photoshop and frameworks such as Foundation for Emails. They've taught me the process of creating, testing, and distributing responsive emails that follow industry standards.
